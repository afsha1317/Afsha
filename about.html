Our Story

From a Tiny Dream

STAMP stands for "S" Shweta, "T" Taskin, "A" Afsha, "M" Madhura, and "P" Pratiksha. It started as a small coffee corner in 2023 and has grown into a beloved community spot. We always believed that a good cup of coffee can bring people together.

Crafting Every Cup

Our beans are locally roasted, our pastries are handmade, and our love for brewing is what makes us different. Every sip you take is part of our story.

A Community Space

More than a cafe, STAMP is a space for artists, friends, and dreamers. We host open mics, art nights, and the best chill vibes in town.
